Welcome to Agrawal Hospital

Where Expert Care
Meets Compassion

Agrawal Hospital is an effort towards providing the best and the latest in diagnosis and treatment of obstetric, gynaecological, respiratory, surgical and critically ill patients in a homely and friendly atmosphere.

Started on 16th August 2002 in the centrally located Arera Colony of Bhopal city with excellent connectivity and easy approachability, we have grown into a trusted name that generates confidence amongst patients. Having treated over 1.5 lakh patients, we have saved more than 350 lives requiring mechanical ventilation. We provide health services to patients from all walks of life and from across the country.

Founder & Director

Dr. P. N. Agrawal

MBBS (Gold Medalist), MD (Medicine), DM (Pulmonary & Critical Care Medicine, PGI Chandigarh), FCCP (USA)

An enterprising and enthusiastic Respiratory & Critical Care Expert practicing for over 30 years. A topper and gold medalist in MBBS & MD courses, he was selected for the prestigious 3-year DM super-speciality course in Pulmonary & Critical Care Medicine at PGI Chandigarh in 1996.

Dr. P. N. Agrawal was the first DM in this specialization in Madhya Pradesh & Chhattisgarh. He has changed the concept of Pulmonary Medicine in the state. Member of M.P. COVID-19 Technical Advisory Committee appointed by the state government.

Our Facilities & Services

Modern infrastructure designed for patient comfort and optimal medical outcomes.

Hospital Wards
Deluxe private (fully AC), semi-private and general wards equipped to provide safe, personalized care with advanced monitoring.
Intensive Care Unit
Each bed equipped with central monitor, central oxygen, central suction for constant monitoring and complex respiratory support.
