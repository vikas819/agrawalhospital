Founder & Director

Dr. P. N. Agrawal

MBBS (Gold Medalist), MD (Medicine), DM (Pulmonary & Critical Care Medicine, PGI Chandigarh), FCCP (USA)

An enterprising and enthusiastic Respiratory & Critical Care Expert practicing for over 30 years. A topper and gold medalist, he was selected for the prestigious DM course at PGI Chandigarh in 1996.

Dr. P. N. Agrawal was the first DM in this specialization in Madhya Pradesh & Chhattisgarh. He has changed the concept of Pulmonary Medicine in the state. Member of M.P. COVID-19 Technical Advisory Committee.

He has performed more than 10,000 Fiber-Optic Video Bronchoscopies and removed more than 350 Airway Foreign Bodies, mostly in children, using Rigid Bronchoscope. He is a guest faculty for Doordarshan and other television channels.
